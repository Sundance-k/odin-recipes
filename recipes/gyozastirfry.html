Goyoza Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • Aldi Stir Fry Kit
  • Aldi 12 Frozen Goyoza
  • 1 Red Onion
  • Finely chopped Spring Onion for garnish
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vegan Noodles
  • Sweet Chilli Sauce

Method

  1. Place the frozen Goyoza in pan with tepid water to expedite thawing (can warm up)
  2. Meanwhile chop the red onion and spring onions and warm some oil in a frying pan
  3. Add the red onion to soften
  4. Check the Goyoza, when reasonably thawed, drain and add some oil to the pan and lightly fry
  5. Meanwhile add the stir fry kit to the frying pan and cook for a few mins
  6. Now add the noodles with a little water and cook for a further 2 mins while stirring
  7. Finally stir in some soy sauce and sweet chilli sauce, serve into bowls and garnish with the spring onion and plate the goyoza on the side with some additional soy if needed
